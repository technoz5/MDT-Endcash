What Do We Do?

At the Ministry of Digital Transformation, we are committed to reshaping the way goods and services are delivered and consumed across the nation. By leveraging appropriate digital technologies, we aim to create a smarter, more connected society.


Our Pillars

  • Digital Society – Empowering citizens through access to technology and information.
  • Digital Government – Streamlining public services with efficiency and transparency.
  • Digital Economy – Enabling businesses and innovation through digital platforms.

Our Core Values

We are guided by the values of capacity development, responsiveness, hope, trust, credibility, transparency, and participation.


Our Commitment

We have the passion and resources to build a digital nation—for our youth, our underserved communities, our citizens and visitors, the national treasury, and all our internal and external stakeholders.


Together, we move forward—digitally.
